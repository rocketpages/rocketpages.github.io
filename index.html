Introduction

I’m a systems analyst, architect, and technical speaker from Toronto, Canada. I specialize in systems modernization, reactive systems architectures, and developer productivity.

I currently work for Lightbend (formerly Typesafe), the corporate entity behind the Scala programming language, Akka distributed systems framework, Play web framework, and Lagom microservices framework.

In my role at Lightbend I work as an analyst and advisor to major enterprises (commonly referred to as the Global 2000 by Forbes) on systems modernization projects using the principles of reactive programming.
